Azure Selfie and Seek - How to Play

Important: You must have an active Twitter account in order to play "Azure Selfie and Seek". Only people who are registered and confirmed can play.

Follow @YOURTWITTER on Twitter so you can get updates as the day progresses.

Registration

  1. Register by sending a selfie to @YOURTWITTER with #rego in the message (in the below image @BitWhere is the account used for the game).


     
  2. Come to the stand at the event and we will confirm your registration.

Playing the game

  1. @YOURTWITTER will announce when a new round begins by tweeting, for example "New round beginning!".


     
  2. We'll give you a hint of who the new hidden person is at the stand.


     
  3. Once you think you know who it is:
    1. find them
    2. ask their permission to take a photo
    3. take a photo of just their head (you increase your chances of winning if the face is clear!)
    4. tweet it to @YOURTWITTER with #found in the message. Sample below:


Winning the game

  1. @YOURTWITTER will announce when a winning entry has been received.


     
  2. Come by the stand to see if you are the winner and collect your prize!
